Overview
golden rules
  • 🚱 Never drink tap water — bottled only.
  • 💵 Exchange cash at authorized counters only; count it yourself, never hand it back.
  • 🛂 Indian passports can't use airport autogates — expect up to 2 hrs at immigration.
  • 🛵 Helmet always; International Driving Permit legally required to ride.
  • 🔌 Plug type C & F (EU round pins), 230V.
  • 🏛️ Temples need covered shoulders + knees.
  • 📵 Don't flash your phone near traffic (scooter snatch-and-go).

Emergency 112 · Tourist Police 110 · Halodoc app = doctor on call.

🌿 Ubud

Days 1–3
🛏️ Stay: Airbnb — Jalan Made Lebah, Kecamatan Ubud, Bali 80571
Host: Annisaa · 📞 +62 812-1063-075
Ubud
Day 1 · arrival

Land 11:00 → immigration ~1.5–2h → drive ~1.5h → reach Airbnb ~3–4 PM. Treat Day 1 as chill + one evening outing.

  • Stroll Ubud center / Ubud Palace, dinner at a veg spot.
  • 🛒 First Indomaret/Alfamart run: big water, snacks, Norit (Bali-belly tablets), Soffell (repellent).
Ubud
Day 2 · sights + ATV
  • Tegallalang Rice Terraces — go early, before tour buses (+ swing if you want the shot).
  • ATV / jungle ride through rivers — or the quirky Jungle Cart Bali engine-free carts 380k ($22).
  • Tirta Empul purification temple — or quieter Beji Griya waterfall (guided) to dodge crowds.
  • Evening: Campuhan Ridge Walk at golden hour, dinner.

⚠️ Ubud traffic worst 12:00–16:00 — do outdoor stuff early.

Ubud
Day 3 · Mt Batur sunrise

~02:00 pickup. Mid-range shared trek 400–600k pp incl. pickup, guide, summit breakfast. Back ~10–11 AM. Optional hot springs after.

Afternoon = REST before the travel day. Pack tonight.

Ubud
getting around

Grab/Gojek work but pickup is restricted in the center. Hire a private driver for the day 600–800k (split 5 ways = tiny).

Ubud
veg eats

Madhuban (100% veg Indian, top pick) · loads of vegan/veg cafes in the center · Satoorasi Coffee (rice-terrace views, N. Ubud).

Ubud
watch out

🐒 At Monkey Forest: no loose sunglasses, bottles or food — monkeys grab everything.

🏝️ Gili Trawangan

Days 4–5
🛏️ Stay: Mad Monkey Hostel (party hostel, south strip)
Gili T
Day 4 · travel + arrive

Morning fast boat (book with Ubud pickup). Arrive early afternoon.

  • No vehicles on the island — cidomo (horse cart) or bike to the hostel.
  • Sunset on the west side (20-min walk/bike across) is unmissable.
  • Evening: beach bars + night market (veg-friendly stalls).
Gili T
Day 5 · snorkel / scuba
  • Snorkel boat tour (turtles at Gili Meno, underwater statues, coral) 150–250k pp. Morning = calmer + clearer.
  • Scuba discovery dive 600k–1.2M — Gili is a top dive spot.
  • Bike the island loop; evening foam/beach parties.
Gili T
getting around

No motor vehicles at all. Walk, rent a bicycle, or take a cidomo. The island's small enough to walk.

Gili T
veg eats

Persona (Indian) · night-market stalls (cheap fresh nasi/mie goreng — say "tanpa ayam/telur").

Gili T
watch out

🏧 ATMs on Gili are unreliable — bring enough cash from Bali. 🌊 Strong currents between islands — snorkel only where guided.

💎 Nusa Penida

Days 6–7
🛏️ Stay: Lushy Hostel (north Nusa area)
Nusa Penida
your tightest stretch — plan it

Diamond Beach is far east (~1h on rough roads). Arriving by boat ~13:30 on D6 leaves little time, so split it: chill D6, do the east run early D7.

Nusa Penida
Day 6 · arrive

Take the 11:00 direct boat from Gili T (~2h) → Banjar Nyuh ~13:30. Check in.

  • Do a near/north sunset (Crystal Bay).
  • Arrange your driver/scooters + early start for tomorrow tonight.
Nusa Penida
Day 7 · EAST run (early!)

Leave ~06:00 with driver:

  • Diamond Beach + Atuh Beach — early beats crowds AND high tide (the beach shrinks/disappears at high tide).
  • Thousand Islands Viewpoint + Rumah Pohon treehouse nearby.
  • Back to port ~11:00–11:30 → boat to Sanur → car to Canggu.

🦖 Kelingking ("T-Rex") is WEST — it won't fit with the east run. Go east as planned.

Nusa Penida
getting around

No Grab/Gojek. Hire a car + driver 600–800k for the group (rough/steep roads), or scooters for confident riders only.

Nusa Penida
veg eats

India Gate & Dhaba (Indian/veg). Limited island — plan meals around your route.

Nusa Penida
watch out

Roads are rough/steep. Check tide times for Diamond Beach. Carry cash — limited ATMs.

Days 7–8

Host: Iluh · 📞 +62 878-6336-3704
"North Kuta" = the Canggu/Kerobokan zone, not Kuta Beach — Finns & Mexicola ~15 min away.
Canggu
Day 8 · beach clubs + nightlife
  • Day: cafe-hop Canggu/Berawa, beach time (Berawa/Echo Beach), shopping.
  • 🍹 Finns Beach Club (Berawa): happy hour 2-for-1 cocktails ~$8; book party-lagoon beds for views; there's a minimum bed spend; bring ID + sunscreen. Alts: Atlas, La Brisa, The Lawn.
  • 🌙 Night: Motel Mexicola (Seminyak/Canggu) — the consistently-recommended club. Late-night: Sand Bar (Canggu beach).
Canggu
getting around

Grab + Gojek work great here — cheapest option; 2 cars for 5 people.

Canggu
veg eats

Indian 100%-veg in Kuta: Ayodhya, Vrindavan (near Kuta Beach), Gujarat. Canggu = endless veg/vegan cafes.

Canggu
shopping

Seminyak: Elce Swim (bikinis), Mia Bali (jewelry), Love Bali Tees, Religion (streetwear). Souvenirs: Krisna Oleh-Oleh (Kuta).

🛕 Uluwatu

Day 9 → 10
🛏️ Stay: Gatsby Hotel Hostel
Uluwatu
Day 9 · temple + Kecak + beaches
  • Drive down (~1–1.5h), check in.
  • Beaches: Padang Padang, Bingin, Suluban (cliff caves). Single Fin = legendary sunset bar/cliff (big party on Sundays).
  • 🎭 Uluwatu Temple sunset + Kecak fire dance — daily ~6 PM, tickets ~150k pp. Arrive ~5 PM for sunset + seats.

🐒 Hold your sunglasses & phone — the temple monkeys are pros.

Uluwatu
Day 10 · fly home

Uluwatu → airport ~30–45 min. Leave 3+ hrs before international departure (traffic + exit immigration). Last coconut 🥥.

Uluwatu
veg eats

Uluwatu cafes: Artisan (brunch), Ours Cafe (lunch), Bambu (healthy), Alchemy (acai/vegan), Banana Lounge (dinner).

🛂 Essentials

Essentials
visa & entry

VoA ≈ IDR 500k / ₹2,700 pp. Do e-VoA + e-Customs + arrival card online before you fly.

⚠️ Arrival card is FREE — only use official allindonesia.imigrasi.go.id. "Service fee" sites are scams.

Essentials
SIM / data

Cheapest = eSIM via Trip.com before landing, or balisim.com (code KHUSHBOO10 = 10% off). Skip the marked-up airport SIM desks.

Essentials
money

Use bank/mall ATMs (cover PIN). Exchange at rated counters (Ace, BMC, Central Kuta). Many warungs are cash only.

⚠️ Stay Safe

Stay Safe
scams
  • Currency: authorized counters only; count it yourself; never hand cash back to be "recounted".
  • Ride-hailing: some drivers demand 5–10x cash after accepting — refuse, cancel, rebook at app price.
  • ATM skimming: bank/mall ATMs only; cover PIN.
  • Fake "arrival card fee" sites — only the official imigrasi site.
  • "Gates of Heaven" photo: the water is a mirror trick; ~1.5h wait, 5 poses. Decide if worth it.
Stay Safe
health
  • Bali belly: carry Norit (charcoal, ~30–60k) + electrolytes. Eat busy places, bottled water only. Halodoc app = doctor/meds to your room.
  • Dengue: Soffell repellent dawn/dusk; long sleeves near rice fields; AC rooms with screens.
  • Tap water can dry out hair/skin — don't ingest it.
  • Strong sun — reef-safe sunscreen, reapply.
Stay Safe
ocean & roads

Strong rip currents at south beaches & between Gilis — swim where flagged. Scooters: Nusa & Gili roads rough/steep — confident riders only, helmet, carry your IDP.

Stay Safe
temple etiquette

Sarong + sash, covered shoulders/knees; don't stand above someone praying.
